A Single Plum Floating in Perfume Served in a Man’s Hat

"I’d like a single plum floating in perfume, served in a man’s hat."

There's more than just Duff and Red Tick Beer on the beverage list at Moe's Tavern.

Ingredients

  • Single Plum
  • A man's hat
  • Perfume

Instructions

  1. Line your hat with plastic wrap. Fill to the top of the plastic with the fragrance of your choice.
  2. Place plum in the hat and…look at it, I guess? Do NOT drink this!
  3. Also, do NOT eat the plum afterward.
