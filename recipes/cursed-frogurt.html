Cursed Frogurt

"The toppings contain potassium benzoate... That’s bad."

If you can't buy a toy, at least buy a forbidden object from places men fear to tread. Or a frozen yogurt, aka frogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons Honey
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Combine yogurt with honey and vanilla extract. Whisk to combine and freeze for at least one hour.
  2. After removing from the freezer, whisk again to remove any ice crystals and serve with your choice of toppings.
  3. Draw a pentagram on your floor and invoke a chant to Satan, asking that a curse be placed on the yogurt.
  4. Sprinkle with your choice of toppings. Enjoy!
